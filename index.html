What is Cubie?

Cubie Messenger (@CubieApp) is a free messaging app that lets you happily draw something funny while you’re chatting. You can also share video, photos and voice messages.
You can download Cubie at: Android Google Play, iPhone App Store

Team

Cubie was founded in 2012 with a small team based in Taiwan. We are one of the 500 Startups family.

Cubie Functions at a glance

  • 1-to-1 chatting
  • Group chat for up to 100 members
  • Drawing tool, for creating artwork or doodling on photos
  • Cubie Space - public gallery for posting artwork
  • Facebook, Instagram and Twitter (Android) sharing functions
  • Voice messaging
  • Video messaging
  • Over 120 static ‘standard’ emoticons (emoji)
  • Over 20 animated sticker sets
  • Interchangeable chatroom backgrounds
  • Syncs with phone’s contact list
  • Sync with Facebook
  • People you may know function
  • Unique Cubie ID
  • Tell a friend (email or SMS)
  • Changeable profile pictures
